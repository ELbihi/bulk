The Split — 6 days

Each muscle 2×/week. Rest 2–3 min compounds, 60–90 s isolation. Day 7 = full rest, non-negotiable.

Progression rules

Double progression: hit the top of the rep range on every set → +2.5 kg next session.
RPE 8–9: keep 1–2 reps in the tank, except final dropsets on pump days → to failure.
Deload week 6: same exercises, 60 % load, half the sets. Skip it and you stall.
Stall = fork problem: lifts frozen 3+ weeks at target kcal → add 200–300 kcal, not more sets.

The meal plan

Breakfast ~700: 3 eggs + khobz + msemen or oats with milk + banana + peanut butter
Lunch ~800: 150 g dry rice/pasta + 200 g chicken or viande hachée + veg + olive oil
Pre-workout ~400: dates + lben/milk + almonds, or sandwich thon
Post-workout ~350: whey + milk + banana (or milk + dates)
Dinner ~700: tagine/couscous with meat, or lentils + eggs + bread
Before bed ~150: raib/yogurt or whole milk

Cheap staples: eggs, sardines, lentils, rice, oats, whole milk, dates, peanut butter, chicken legs. Liquid calories are your friend.
